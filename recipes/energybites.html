Energy Bites Recipe

Image credit: The Minimalist Baker

Description

These energy bites are delicious and perfect for bringing on a hike or a long drive. They are vegan and easy to make gluten-free

Ingredients

  • 1.25 C oats
  • .5 C peanut butter
  • 2.5T chia seeds
  • 2T flax seeds
  • .25C raisins
  • .25C maple syrup

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients
  2. Let rest in fridge for 5 minutes
  3. Form into balls
  4. Enjoy!
