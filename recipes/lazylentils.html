Lazy Lentils Recipe

Photo credit: Prevention.com

Description

Lentils are delicious and healthy. This is an extremely simple way to prepare them.

Ingredients

  • 1C lentils
  • 2.5C water
  • .25t salt
  • 1T olive oil
  • 2T ketchup

Steps

  1. Add water and lentils to pot; bring to boil, then simmer until lentils are soft
  2. Mix in salt, olive oil, and ketchup
  3. Enjoy!
