Vegan Pasta Recipe

photo credit: Veggie Inspired

Description

This delicious pasta is super simple for the lazy vegan.

Ingredients

  • 1C pasta
  • 4C water
  • .24C nutritional yeast
  • 2T olive oil
  • 1t chopped fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta; boiled to preferred softness
  3. Drain pasta
  4. Add nutritional yeast and olive oil; stir to combine
  5. Add optional basil for garnish
  6. Enjoy!
